# Refund & Cancellation Policy

_Last updated: 24 June 2026_

This policy explains how billing, cancellations and refunds work for **Convorian**
(operated by Shantanu Kulkaarni), the AI WhatsApp assistant for real-estate agents
available at https://convorian.in. By subscribing, you agree to this policy together
with our [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service) and [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

## 1. Free trial

New accounts start on a **free trial — 1 month or 500 assistant messages, whichever
comes first.** No payment is taken during the trial. If you do not subscribe, the
assistant simply stops responding when the trial ends; you are never charged
automatically for the trial itself.

## 2. Subscription & billing

- Convorian is a **monthly subscription** (currently ₹999/month, plus applicable
  taxes; pricing may change with notice for new billing cycles).
- Payments are processed securely through **Razorpay**. Monthly plans renew
  automatically via UPI AutoPay / the mandate you authorise.
- Your subscription gives you access to the service for the **paid billing period**.

## 3. Cancellation

- You may **cancel any time** — from your dashboard or by emailing
  **support@convorian.in**.
- On cancellation, **auto-renewal stops** and you are not charged again.
- Your access **continues until the end of the period you have already paid for** —
  it is not cut off immediately, and the already-paid period is not pro-rated.

## 4. Refunds

Convorian is a digital subscription service. As a rule, **payments for a billing
period already started are non-refundable**, because the service is delivered
continuously through that period.

We will, however, issue a **good-faith refund** in these cases, on request within
**7 days** of the charge:

- a **duplicate or erroneous charge**;
- you were billed **after cancelling** for a period you did not intend to renew;
- a **verified technical failure on our side** that prevented the assistant from
  working for a substantial part of the period and we could not resolve it.

Refunds are approved at Convorian's reasonable discretion after review. WhatsApp /
Meta messaging charges (paid by you directly to Meta where applicable) and taxes
already remitted are not refundable by Convorian.

## 5. How to request a refund

Email **support@convorian.in** (or our Grievance Officer at
**grievance@convorian.in**) with your registered email/number and the payment
reference. Approved refunds are returned to the **original payment method**, usually
within **5–7 business days** after approval (your bank may take longer to reflect it).
