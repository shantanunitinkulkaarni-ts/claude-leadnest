# Refund & Cancellation Policy

_Last updated: 25 June 2026_

This policy explains how billing, cancellations and refunds work for **TING**, the AI
WhatsApp assistant for real-estate agents (operated by **Convorian** / Shantanu
Kulkaarni), available at https://convorian.in. By subscribing, you agree to this
policy together with our [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service) and
[Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

## 1. Free plan

New accounts start on a **free plan** at no cost — it includes a limited allowance
(AI assistant messages, leads and properties) so you can experience the product. No
payment is taken on the free plan. To remove the limits and run the assistant on your
real leads, you subscribe to a paid plan.

## 2. Subscription & billing

- TING paid access is a **subscription** — currently **₹999/month** (plus applicable
  taxes; pricing may change with notice for new billing cycles). Quarterly and annual
  plans may be offered.
- Payments are processed securely through **Razorpay**. Plans renew automatically via
  UPI AutoPay / the mandate you authorise.
- **WhatsApp message charges are billed directly by Meta to your own WhatsApp Business
  account** — they are not collected by Convorian, and are separate from your TING
  subscription.

## 3. Cancellation

- You may **cancel any time** — from your dashboard or by emailing
  **support@convorian.in**.
- On cancellation, **auto-renewal stops** and you are not charged again.
- Your access **continues until the end of the period you have already paid for** — it
  is not cut off immediately, and the already-paid period is not pro-rated.

## 4. Refunds

TING is a digital subscription service delivered continuously.

- **Monthly plans are non-refundable.** Because the monthly term is short and the
  service is delivered continuously, payments for a monthly plan are not refundable.
  If something isn't working, contact us and we'll fix it or you can cancel to stop
  future charges.
- **Quarterly and annual plans** may be refunded **only** where there is a **clear,
  verified malfunction of the assistant that made the service substantially unusable**,
  or a **demonstrable monetary loss caused by a fault on our side**, that we were
  unable to resolve. In such a case we refund the **unused portion** of the longer
  term, at Convorian's reasonable discretion after review.
- WhatsApp / Meta messaging charges (paid by you to Meta) and taxes already remitted
  are **not refundable** by Convorian.

## 5. How to request a refund

Email **support@convorian.in** (or our Grievance Officer at
**grievance@convorian.in**) with your registered email/number, the plan, and the
payment reference. Approved refunds are returned to the **original payment method**,
usually within **5–7 business days** after approval (your bank may take longer to
reflect it).
